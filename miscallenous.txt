Career advice:
1) work on company-wide influential projects
2) work proactively on problems in need of resources
3) ask for promotion, ask for advice
4) talk with folks to get different perspectives
5) find a good mentor



Think of the test plan of a feature In decreasing order of priority:
1) No regression
2) Functionalities
3) Guard against erroneous configuration, extreme cases  
4) Stress/Scale tests (nothing breaks, guards actually work)
